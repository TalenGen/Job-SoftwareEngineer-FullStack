TalenGen Internship

Job Title: Software Engineer (Full Stack)

  • Location : Online
  • Job Type: Internship

About TalenGen:

TalenGen is a startup aiming to become the #1 recruitment platform in Southeast Asia (SEA). We provide a comprehensive suite of networking, job recruiting, live chat, AI matching, and events features on our platform. Our mission is to connect talented individuals with top-notch opportunities and streamline the hiring process for both employers and job seekers.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with CTO, designers, and fellow engineers to understand feature requirements and translate them into technical specifications.
  • Design, develop, test, and deploy end-to-end software solutions that integrate the features of our platform.
  • Build robust and scalable APIs and backend systems to support seamless data flow between different features.
  • Develop intuitive user interfaces (UIs) and front-end components that deliver an exceptional user experience across various devices and platforms.
  • Implement intelligent algorithms and AI models to improve job matching and recommendation capabilities.
  • Ensure the security, performance, and reliability of the platform by optimizing code, conducting thorough testing, and addressing any issues or bugs.
  • Collaborate with the team to continuously improve software development processes, coding standards, and best practices.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest technologies, trends, and industry developments, and incorporate them into the platform to drive innovation.
  • Regularly report project progress to the CTO, including status updates, milestones achieved, and any challenges or risks encountered.

Qualifications:

  • Solid understanding of web application development, including both front-end and back-end technologies.
  • Experience building scalable, performant, and secure web applications.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to analyze complex technical challenges.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or GCP, and experience with deploying applications in a cloud environment.
  • Knowledge of relational and NoSQL databases and their integration in a web application.
  • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git) and agile software development methodologies.
  • Ability to provide clear and concise project updates to the CTO, highlighting progress, achievements, and potential challenges.

Nice to have:

  • Experience with integrating chat systems, AI algorithms, or event management features.
  • Knowledge of machine learning and natural language processing (NLP) techniques.
  • Familiarity with containerization and orchestration tools like Docker and Kubernetes.
  • Understanding of microservices architecture and RESTful API design principles.

Joining TalenGen offers an exciting opportunity to work in a dynamic and fast-paced startup environment, shaping the future of recruitment in SEA. If you are passionate about building innovative software solutions and want to make a significant impact, we would love to hear from you.
